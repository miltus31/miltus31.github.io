Supplementary Material for paper ID:29
----------------------------------------

Each folder contains different images and its corresponding dehazed result by different methods. The file naming convention is described below. 

Filename convention -
<filename>_input - Original Image 
<filename>_tan - Result of Tan's method [1]
<filename>_fattal - Result of Fattal's old method [2]
<filename>_dc - Result of Dark Channel Prior [3]
<filename>_dc_own - Result of Dark Channel Prior, our own implementation
<filename>_cl - Result of Color line based dehazing [4]
<filename>_our - Result of our method
